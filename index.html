Public Employment Service Office

The Public Employment Service Office (PESO) is a non-fee charging, multi-dimensional facility in the Philippines, established under R.A. 8759 (PESO Act of 1999) to provide free employment services, job placement, and career guidance. Operating within Local Government Units (LGUs) and in coordination with DOLE, it serves jobseekers for both local and overseas employment.
